How It Works?

DoubIU is all about enabling you in today's gig economy.You the gig hirer. You the gig worker.

For Hirer

DoubIU is about empowerment, cost control and ease of engagement.

Empowerment to directly hire a worker based on your requirements. Choose your people yourself, don't let your most precious assets be determined by someone who "thinks" he understands what you need.

Cost control with the ability to hire the people you want for exactly the duration you need them for. Complete visibility into availability. Think POC. Think critical fix. Think temporary workload management. No more term based contracts.

Easy of engagement with complete control over the contracting cycle. No more lengthy onboarding processes for every worker. Full transparency in billing.

  • Setup profile with your details and billing information.
  • Search for your perfect candidate by skills and availability. No job postings, no agencies. Simple
  • Shortlist your candidate. choose required dates and get an instant quote. Complete transparency.
  • Confirm dates and directly book your candidate. That's it. All set!
  • Invoices sent at the end of every fortnight. Too easy!

For Worker

DoubIU is about enablement, control and reclaiming life.

Enablement by bringing the jobs to you. No more rummaging through job postings and indiscriminately applying everywhere. Let hiring managers contact you directly. Why depend on "middle men"?

Control to determine what you wish to work on and when. Control over your wages. No more negotiations on salaries, increments and appraisals.

Reclaim life. Work to live. Go on that trip when you want, not when you "can". Take time to learn scuba diving. Spend time with the family. Enrich your market relevance with the certifications and training you want.

  • Setup profile and keep it updated with your skills, rate and availability.
  • Get notified when you are shortlisted or receive a booking request from a hirer. Eliminate having to spend hours scouring for the right jobs.
  • Talk to the hirer, understand the role and accept the booking request only if it meets your expectations. Total control!
  • Start working and have fun!
  • Get paid. Too easy!
