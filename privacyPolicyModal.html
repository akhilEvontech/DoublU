About Us

The future of corporate hiring in the new gig economy. Source Your resources here

Our Mission

Our mission is to help worker and enterprises seek out greater meaning and relevance in flexible employment and hiring in the workplace of tomorrow.

Our Story

At doubIU, we are all about enabling you to claim your independence and power to the way you work and the way you hire. We are committed to developing the doubIU platform to enable the new gig economy through easy for employers seeking highly skilled individuals and for workers seeking out perfect gig.

Motivated by our own frustration in our corporate jobs, we created a technology platform to not only allow easy access and collaboration, but also the freedom and full control to when, where and how much workers to work and for companies to hhire on demand without the cost of hiring, onboarding and administering a full time employee.

Sensitive Information
